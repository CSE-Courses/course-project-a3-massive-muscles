BMI and Body Fat Calculator
Body Mass Index, or BMI, is derived with respect to your weight and height. An abnormal BMI could indicate underlying health issues. We can also calculate your body fat percentage (+/- 3%) using the same standards as the US Navy. In most cases, this process is more accurate than skinfold calipers!
